Masala Omelette

Description

Masala Omelette is a spicy Indian-style omelette made with eggs, onions, tomatoes, green chilies, and fresh herbs. It's quick, flavorful, and perfect for a healthy breakfast or light meal.

Ingridiants

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 small onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 small tomato (finely chopped)
  • 1 green chili (finely chopped)
  • 2 tablespoons fresh coriander leaves (chopped)
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon oil or butter

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and beat them well.
  2. Add chopped onion, tomato, green chili, coriander leaves, salt, turmeric, and red chili powder to the eggs. Mix well.
  3. Heat oil or butter in a pan over medium heat.
  4. Pour the egg mixture into the pan and spread it evenly.
  5. Cook for 2-3 minutes until the bottom is set, then flip and cook the other side.
  6. Once both sides are cooked, remove from the pan and serve hot.
