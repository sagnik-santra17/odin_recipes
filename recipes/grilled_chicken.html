Grilled Chicken

Description

Juicy and flavorful chicken marinated with simple spices, then grilled to perfection. A healthy and delicious dish that's perfect for any meal!

Ingridiants:

  • 2 chicken breasts (boneless, skinless)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano or thyme

Steps:

  1. In a bowl, mix olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, salt, pepper, paprika, chili powder, and oregano.
  2. Place the chicken breasts in the bowl and coat them well with the marinade.
  3. Cover and let it marinate in the fridge for at least 30 minutes (or up to 2 hours for more flavor).
  4. Preheat your grill or grill pan over medium heat.
  5. Place the marinated chicken on the grill.
  6. Cook each side for about 5â€“7 minutes or until the chicken is fully cooked and has grill marks.
  7. Remove from grill and let it rest for a few minutes before serving.
